Taylor Johnson, MLIS

Taylor’s rich professional history in complex data description and organization results in high-quality archival results. With over ten years of experience in libraries, Taylor enjoys working closely with data providers to ensure long-term preservation and interoperability of digitized and born-digital institutional assets. She is passionate about open access, equity, and data reuse.

Good data stewardship is about understanding the whole picture; from point of creation to retrieval. With this in mind, Taylor is committed to providing you with a reliable product, so you can focus on the science.
